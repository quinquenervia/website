Resources

At quinquenervia eco, we believe that information should be easily accessible and freely available. Our aim is to create high-quality and easy-to-understand guides to the living things in our local area, and to direct like-minded individuals to the right sources of information they need. This page serves as a repository for the educational resources we have created, as well as bookmarks that we use or think others with a similar passion may find useful, and keys to help identify species. These pages are constantly evolving, so please don't hesitate to contact us at hello@quinquenervia.com if you encounter any errors or if you would like to contribute to this growing list of resources.
